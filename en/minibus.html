Minibus Service

We can offer a private minibus service at an additional cost. We can arrange door to door pick up for your child or a subsidized pick up from local playgrounds in downtown Budapest. Transport can be provided on a one way or on a return basis. The companies we work with provide modern minibuses and are operated by safety conscious and child-friendly drivers.

On arrival to the Nursery School or Pre-School, the children are collected directly from the minibus by a member of staff and handed over to their teacher; in the afternoon a member of staff delivers them to the minibus, gets them seated and then hands over to the minibus driver. The minibus service is not suitable for babies.

If you are interested in this option, please contact finance@englishgarden.hu detailing your address and the minibus service you require.
