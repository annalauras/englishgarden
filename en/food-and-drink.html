Food and Drink

The English Garden aims to provide a balanced and enjoyable child friendly diet. We offer breakfast for children arriving before 8:30 a.m., morning snack, hot lunch, an afternoon snack, as well as a late snack for children staying after 5.00pm. We endeavor to meet special dietary requirements and offer vegetarian, lactose and dairy free, and egg free options. Parents are welcome to provide their own food if they wish however all meals and snacks are included in our fees.

All children are encouraged to take part in our 5-A-Day programme which is adopted from Britain, this promotes the benefits of eating 5 separate portions of fruit or vegetables per day. Our catering staff will ensure that children are provided with a good variety of fruits and vegetables during meal and snack times.

Mineral water is available for the children from dispensers which they have access to throughout the day. During mealtimes we provide tap water, milk, fruit teas and fruit juices according to the planned daily menu.
